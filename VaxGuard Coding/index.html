Why Vaccinate?

"Vaccines save lives; fear endagers them. A simple message parents need to keep hearing."


Vaccines are the most effective way to prevent many infectious diseases. The global immunisation efforts have saved an estimated 154 million lives or the equivalent of six lives every minute of every year over the past 50 years. The vast majority of lives saved - 101 million, were those of infants.
